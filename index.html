VerseCraft is designed to be a comprehensive, AI-assisted writing platform for both English and Urdu writers. It will allow users to manage their writing workflows by providing tools such as chapter organization, plot and character development, notes creation, and collaborative writing. In addition, it will provide AI-based features such as grammar checking and a chatbot for content improvement.
